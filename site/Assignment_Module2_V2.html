INDIAN RECEIPES

DHOKLA


Indregiants : 3 part rice and 3 part Chana dal. Soak them and grind them. Add salt and chilly. Steam it. It's ready to serve.

IDLI


Indregiants : 3 part rice and 3 part Urad dal. Soak them and grind them. Add salt and chilly. Steam it.It's ready to serve.

MEDUVADA


Indregiants : 3 part Urad dal and 1 part rice. Soak them and grind them. Add salt and chilly. Deepfry it. It's ready to serve.
